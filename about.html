About Our Business & Mission Statement

Lento Auto Repair has been a tribute to Lech Mikiewicz's constant passion and ability in the automotive world since its debut in 2003. Lech moved from Poland with over a decade of hands-on expertise in the automobile sector, particularly with frame repairs and services. His passion for vehicles has matured over the years into a wealth of expertise spanning more than 30 years, embracing a varied spectrum of vehicle types from luxury sedans to utilitarian SUVs. Lento Auto Repair promises to provide top-tier vehicle restoration and maintenance with competitive pricing in the industry, thanks to his experienced team of hand-picked, skilled experts.

Lento Auto Repair's primary premise is its commitment to quality across a wide range of services. The crew goes above and beyond to ensure that every vehicle receives thorough maintenance, from routine tire changes and rotations to complimentary oil changes. Their knowledge extends effortlessly to mechanical and electrical repairs, guaranteeing that no car problem is beyond their reach. Furthermore, their expertise in painting services gives a bit of delicacy to any restoration or repair operation.
